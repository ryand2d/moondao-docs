## Holder Roles
- **[[@Member]]:** Holder of any amount of locked MOONEY. Can vote on MoonDAO proposals.

## Contributor Roles
- **[@Project Contributor](@Project%20Contributor.md):** Currently working within a MoonDAO project.
- **[@Project Lead](@Project Lead.md):** Leader of a MoonDAO project.
- **[[@Executive Lead]]:** Leader of MoonDAO.
- **@Treasury Signer:** Signer on MoonDAO’s Treasury.
- **[@Voyager](@Voyager.md):** Former Project Contributor, Project Lead, Executive Lead, or Treasury Signer.
- **[[@Alien]]:** A verified member of our Discord.
